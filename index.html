Shalini in a Nutshell!!!.

A Short story time..!!

Once upon a time, a young professional landed her very first job — at one of the most prestigious banks in the country.

She worked on high-impact, compliance-driven systems, ensuring that critical data processes ran smoothly and securely.
It was serious work. Responsible work. But something felt... missing.

She loved working with data — but not just to report things. She wanted to see them. To play with them. To paint insights in color.

But her role didn’t allow that.

So, she made a choice. A quiet one at first. She began her own journey into Data Science.

At night. On weekends. With curiosity as fuel.
She taught herself Python, SQL, and the art of asking better questions.

Soon, she was doing things she once only imagined:

  • Cleaned messy datasets and brought stories to life
  • Built 5+ full-fledged EDA projects that made trends speak loud and clear
  • Trained ML models with up to 85% accuracy — and yes, she tuned them herself
  • Created a growing Kaggle & GitHub portfolio that shows her code, her thinking, and her growth
  • Designed interactive dashboards using Power BI and Streamlit

This isn’t just a story about shifting careers.
This is a story about finding where you belong —
and then building your way there,
one dataset at a time.
